PinkCards.com - Quality and Service Checklist
Thanks for your interest in Pinkcards.com

We haven't got round to completing all our documentation and process yet. We will get back to you with the Services & Quality guidelines in the near future. Until then, an outline of the services we are looking to achieve from each partner is as follows :

  1. Print using a 600 dpi SRA3 digital press using NON oil based fusers / toners, preferably Xerox 700/J75, 800/1000, iGen or Konica Bizhub 6/7/8000 or equivalent.
  2. All Business cards to be printed on 310gsm Satin Art Board.
  3. Replace/ Re-sharpen Guillotine blades after cutting 20,000 sheet, ensure cutting sticks are well maintained
  4. Gloss Lamination thickness : 28-40 micron, ensure suitable adhesion to digital prints.
  5. Matt Lamination thickness : 28-40 micron, ensure suitable adhesion to digital prints.
  6. 5mm Round cornering capability : TBD (optional)
  7. Adhere to Job cards Instructions and Quantities
  8. Adhere to printing and packaging standards (TBD)
  9. Double check all artwork by sight even tho electronically proofed by end user.
  10. Check for obvious spelling mistakes even tho electronically proofed by end user
  11. Check for text mis-alignments and spacing even tho electronically proofed by end user
  12. Check the tolerances for Bleed and Trimming are sufficient and at a comfortable level to print and trim
  13. Check the print resolution of images and pictures even tho electronically proofed by end user.
  14. Adhere to Adobe PDF print engine recommendations and profile - http://www.adobe.com/products/pdfprintengine 
  15. Revert back to the end user directly and recommend any improvements to final artwork before going to print.
  16. Revert back to the end user directly if estimated despatch date is going to over run - Customer should be warned 24hours before estimated despatch date.
  17. Adhere to best endeavours to print and delver within the specified estimated dispatch date + 1 Day.
